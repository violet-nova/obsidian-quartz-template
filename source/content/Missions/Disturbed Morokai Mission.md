

## Initial Summary

> *Upon delivery of one large Bobulator Tractor to Andy Wheatwhisk in Whitbourne, you are surprised to see a swarm of TSUNAMI personnel have set up a perimeter around his farm and – as their leader Gwen Alco explains– are allowing nobody, including Andy or his co-owner, Emmet Oatbrook into the farm. 
> 
> This is to protect from a Morokai, a fauna that exists somewhere in between a nonmagical tiger and a tiger spirit, that has wandered into the middle of the farm and is intermittently displaying aggression.
> 
> *With the Morokai visibly channeling the energy of farm plants (a few of which now look shriveled and decayed), there’s concern its reach will spread to surrounding areas. TSUNAMI are currently just observing, but it’s suspected they will bomb the site to prevent a town-wide infestation. 
> 
> *The farm features grass of varying heights (from the tall cornfields to the modest pastures), a barnhouse, a few other small animal pens, and opaque violet crystal formations, the tallest of which span a few feet wide and must be at least ten feet tall. 
> 
> *Goal: Find a way to end the standoff, ideally with nobody getting hurt.


## How it Went Down

[[Jungo Jargon]] convinced [[Commander Gwen Alco]] that he, [[Finley]], [[Kal]],  and [[Zyx]] were also [[TSUNAMI]] and wanted to help.  

Zyx established contact with the Morokai, [[Kausi]], and convinced it to let the Mailers approach.

[[Ed Hammer]] was lurking in a nearby tree with a noisemaker that disturbed the Morokai. Ed  threatened Zyx and Kausi at gunpoint once Zyx determined this. But Finley, Kal, and Jungo successfully intimidated Ed into dropping his weapon. They tied him up. 

[[2D]] used his eye for terrain to expertly slip through tall grass toward the middle of the farm, but ran into [[Amy Nementia]]. Not wanting a young person to get mixed up in the danger, 2D led Amy back to the perimeter of the farm. 

Zyx, Finley, Kal, and Jungo all saw that Kausi was protecting two rare emerald moonshrike eggs. Zyx saw through Kausi's memories that two TSUNAMI agents had shot moonshrikes near the farm, causing the Morokai to appear, and that the Morokai had carried the eggs back to the middle of the farm, then paced trying to think of an escape plan before being disturbed by Hammer's noisemaker. 

Zyx convinced Kausi to leave the farm if the group smuggled the eggs  out so that TSUNAMI would not discover them. Jungo radioed Gwen and convinced her to let Kausi leave. 

Elsewhere, 2D learned that Amy was a member of Synapse Wreath curious about the Morokai, and attempted to deflect her interest from both Morokai and Mailers alike. Nevertheless, Amy picked up on the group's location and recalled their [Tamaris Telescope](The%20Tamaris%20Telescope.md) profile when 2D mentioned [[Sipfest]]. 

The Mailers successfully snuck the eggs out and Kausi successfully left the farm. Before departing via ship, Finley placed a device in one of the perpetrating TSUNAMI soldiers' vehicle. He was not seen doing the deed, but attracted suspicion for lurking near where the vehicles were parked. 

The Mailers rendezvoused with Kausi in a Crystal Cradle Moonshrike cave, where they witnessed the Moonshrike babies hatching from the eggs. 

Andy Wheatwhisk compensated the Mailers for restoring peace to his farm.
