
## Setup
A citizen of Haletown petitioned the Mailers to takedown [[Corvin Hale]] after recieving a tip her father would soon be laid off. Employees of Haletown are typically trafficked to the Red Empire and enslaved as prisoners. 


## How it Went Down

The Mailers infiltrated Wright's skyscraper posing as laborers. They successfully convinced [[Lio Finch]] of their legitimacy and got to working their way further up.

[[Finley]] hacked an elevator.

[[Kal]] struck up a conversation with [[Davin Kest]] at the bar, and convinced him to take the elevator up, where Finley ambushed him and took his ID card. Finley, [[2D]], [[Jungo Jargon]], and [[Zyx]] used the keycard to enter the residential area. They spilled booze on Kest and subtly moved the body to his apartment. Jungo stayed behind to divert security by bluffing a snake attack. Inside Kest's apartment, Zyx found a grappling hook and details of getting higher up.

Zyx and Jungo headed up through the vents. 

Elsewhere, [[Kal]] convinced [[Mara Cineglass]]  to take the elevator up and took her place as secretary briefly, before heading out to look for potential exits. 

Capitalizing on Kal's movement of Cineglass, Finley and Jungo leapt across elevators, rode up with Cineglass, and came in through the elevator roof when Cineglass got off in Administration.

Zyx and Jungo bluffed their way past some maintenance workers and rendezvoused with 2D and Finley in Administration. 

Zyx stole [[Celian Marrow]]'s keycard to floor 49 while Jungo distracted them. 

The group headed up to floor 49 and went out a window to climb up to floor 50 using jump kits. 

Meanwhile, Kal met Nima Sol and began instigating a laborers' riot. 

Zyx, Jungo, Finley, and 2D made it up to the penthouse. Finley hacked into Corvus's system with help from 2D and Jungo. Zyx cracked the safe, finding Corvus's ledger. The group hid when Corvus came up and slipped back down to floor 49.

Finley sent Corvus crashing down the elevator to the bottom of the building and the group slid down the ropes in the elevator shaft, pursued by a killer butler.

Kal had gotten the laborers to riot but was injured in the process. Zyx made her way to him and patched him up amidst the chaos. The group sprinted for the windows and slid their way down to the ground floor, then hightailed it to their vehicle. Zyx was badly hurt on the way down.
