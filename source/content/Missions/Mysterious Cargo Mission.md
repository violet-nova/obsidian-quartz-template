
## Initial Summary

> *Tobias Kren, Head of Southern Shipping from the Meridian Coalition wants you to deliver some important crates to a Coalition clerk’s house in Harrowfin, a town located within the southern half of the domain of Lunaline. The cargo crate reads “Do not open. Do not delay” in large font. (You are advised that these are orders, not suggestions.)  In smaller font: “Durable, but not indestructible”.

> *To make it in time, you need to pass through the Razor Channel, an area known for Glasswind Storms. Glasswind storms are a lot like sandstorms, but with large chunks of silica, but they can happen anywhere with lots of silica. (The region has a ton of radiant era tech and silicate lifeforms) Commonly, people are warned to duck/wear protective gear to minimize lacerations, and that the singing of the storm can cause hearing damage or interfere with technical equipment. This in addition to the usual considerations of strong winds and challenges to visibility.

>*It’s also speculated the crate could have caught the interest of imperial spies. You have orders to attack immediately if anyone attacks you, tries to grab the crate, or is observably an imperial spy.*

> *And you are more generally worried about who else might take an interest… media, mobs, random passerby.*


## How it Went Down

[[Jungo Jargon]] negotiated passage with [[The Passage Brothers]] in exchange for an invite to [[Sipfest]]. 

The Mailers expertly navigated the brutal Glassstorm. [[Finley]] handled the steering, [[Kal]] and [[Zyx]] did many repairs, and [[2D]] maintained lookout.

[[Ash Voss]] pursued the group, but they escaped and damaged Ash's ship with their guns. 

[[Jungo Jargon]] persuaded [[Sable Kent]] to stop asking about their cargo in exchange for a profile on the Crew. 

The cargo was successfully delivered to Harrowfin. After some time with the cargo in their private office out of sight of the Mailers, the official paid the Mailers and told them they were free to return the cargo to Mirrorwood at their convenience.
