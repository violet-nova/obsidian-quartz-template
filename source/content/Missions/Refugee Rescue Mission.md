
## The Setup

The Mailers were hired by [[The Leafneedles]] to get some refugees out of the [[Red Empire]] and into [[Pinelocke]]. They were advised that:
 * getting out of the town may be challenging
 * Pinelocke's mountain ranges are treacherous -- snowstorms are a constant and making good time requires following a path of short range portals that temporarily open
 * snow griffons are known to sometimes get territorial
	 * Often they will want to parlay with ships -- they may ask for trinkets, stories, riddles, jokes, food, or anything.
* With 14 people on board a long term journey, at least one full restock of food and water would be necessary.

## How it Went Down

The Mailers entered the town of Sleetstar where the refugees were and posed as agricultural shippers. They completed a few deliveries to build credibility with the Red Empire guards. 

Zyx and 2D played with the catboys, Petyr Jr and Pterry briefly at the safehouse.

The Mailers installed a false bottom in the truck beds.  Linda and James, the Red Empire guards, were convinced.

Safely out of Red Empire territory, the refugees mingled with the Mailers. Pterry and Peter were given tinfoil captain's hats. 

[[Zyx]] modified the comms so that Jungo could communicate with the [[Snow Unicorn]]. The Unicorn was diving through portals in the Mountains 
![[Pasted image 20260716214740.png]]

The Mailers parlayed with [[Iris]], a [[snow griffon]]. [[Jungo Jargon]] offered her a choice, and she chose to have [[2D]] recite a riddle ("What is orange and sounds like a parrot? A carrot.")

Iris chose to stay on board with the group to take a break from flying. Her two children stayed as well

[[2D]] and Iris went up in [[Big Bird]]'s telescoping crow's nest. With Iris's spiritual assistance, 2D clearly saw a hyperspecific path the ship could take to avoid damage and make good enough time to avoid the portals closing on them. 

To restock food, 2D and Finley went down in Little Birds with Iris to hunt and gather in a wooded area. They scavenged more than enough but were spotted and studied by [[The Order of Mottus]]. [[Alfred Mottus]] conversed with 2D briefly while Finley hid in his Little Bird. The trio returned to the ship, the order following at a respectable distance.

Jungo Jargon modified a Little Bird to collect snow for boiling and fitted a funnel onto the ship for ongoing snow collection. Plenty was gathered, but the weathershield of the Little Bird was damaged.
