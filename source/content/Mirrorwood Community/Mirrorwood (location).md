
## Location
Mirrorwood is an underground cave-town in the Meridian Valley, located near a distributary to the east of the Crystal Cradle.

## Appearance/Geography

### Light Source
Mirrorwood gets artifical sunlight from its Lake, and as such does not need direct sunlight.

The light casts a lovely purple during the day and turns red at night. 

### Housing
The residents live in trees that grow from the cave ceiling, above the lake. Hovertech is used to traverse from house to house.

It is considered unusual to allow visitors in the treetop houses.

### Nightmarket

Near the lake are ruins that residents use as a nightmarket for visitors. 

### Lakewater

The Mirrorwood Lakewater isn't safe to drink. In small doses it can inebriate and cause skin to glow purple; in larger doses it can sicken or fatally poison the drinker.
