Lilah is the daughter of an official from Junivale and a member of Mirrorwood.

## Pronouns: She/Fae

## Motifs
* Dynamic hairstyle
* Deep-set purple eyes
* tiny pocketwatch

## Foundation: Semiosis Training Facility

Lilah runs a combination coffee shop and entertainment venue. It features recurring open mic nights where social skills can be practiced.


## Faction Relationships

### Meridian Coalition (+1)
Though she considers her flight from Junivale an act of personal rebellion against her father, she's still on good terms with the [[Meridian Coalition]].


## Notable Interactions with Mailers

Lilah was the one who set up the [[Mysterious Cargo Mission]] and has been looking after the cargo since the aftermath.
