The Order of Mottus are a group of researchers concerned with cataloguing the rare species, humanity (adapated, radiant, and synthetic).

Most Order members are [[SnowPo]]s, but at least one is a giraffe.

## VItals
* **Tier: 3**
* Location: Pinelocke wilderness near the mountains
