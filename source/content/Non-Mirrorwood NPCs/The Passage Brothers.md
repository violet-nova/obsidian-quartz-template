**Names:** Marky, RIcky, Danny, Terry, Tony, and Brian (all he/him)
**Motifs:** Opportunistic, Argumentative, Softer than they project

The six "Passage Brothers" are an autonomous group that guard the northern mouth of a narrow valley in the Meridian Valley Wilderness via a guard tower. 
### Known Relationships
* Ricky's son
* Each other

## Location

The post they guard is south of the Crystal Cradle and west of Lunaline. 


## Significance of Post

The valley the Passage Brothers guard provides shelter from Glasswind Storms. 

For a number of reasons, the valley is not frequently sought out by travelers:
* Though they typically last between 5-10 days, Glasswind Storms only occur 1-3 times per calendar year. When it is not storming, travellers can simply avoid the valley.
* The valley isn't of use to those traveling east-west or vice versa.
* Even for north-south travellers on storm days, a slightly longer route through Lunaline is usually preferred -- little time is added, and the hospitality of the commmunities is highly sought after.

However, sometimes north-south travellers on tight deadlines can't avoid a storm, and that's when the Brothers' Passage becomes indispensable. 

## Pricing

Typically the Brothers only ask for small favors in exchange for passage. Invites to community events, samples of exotic foods, a single game of dragonchess, and "some insight as to how to strike up a conversation with Miss Cineglass" are all examples reported by travellers.
