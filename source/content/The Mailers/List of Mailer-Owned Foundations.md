
## Speciality

The group has a **Carrier truck**, a heavy hovertruck equipped with hovering pallettes. [It's an 18-wheeler](Big%20Bird.md) 

## Prosperity 1

* **Backchatter Network** -- A group of rumor gatherers who report to [[Lloyd Mattison]]
* **Certified Licensing** -- Official permission to ship in the Valley, provided by Coalition beuaracrats and maintained through the recordkeeping of [[Philip]].
* **Radiant Archive** -- A library full of scientific texts that survived the cataclysm. [[Mamo]] is an enthusiastic volunteer and patron.
* **Semiosis Training**  -- A coffee shop and entertainment venue that serves drinks at night. [[Lilah Wren]] manages it.
* **Playbook Training** -- A Lounge for the Mailers connected to specialized rooms. There are Bonsai trees for [[2D]], a room full of CRT TVs for [[Jungo Jargon]], a witchy shrine in a dark corner for [[Zyx]], a punching bag for [[Kal]], and a toolbench for [[Finley]].
