## Appearance
Finley is an adapted human. He is about four feet tall, with slender arms and fingers but stocky legs. He has extraordinarily large eyes and is covered in fine blue hair.

## Abilities
Finley is a [[Scrapper]], a savvy technician and mechanic.
### Playbook abilities
* **Hotfix** - When you spend harmony on a roll to repair, modify, upgrade, or sabotage technology, you gain +1 impact. When you work on a synthesis project during downtime, your roll adds +1 tick to the project clock.
* **Controlled chaos** -  When you sabotage technology or infrastructure, you can choose to keep your work quiet and well-hidden from casual inspection.

## Early life in Corundrum
Finley's very young years were spent in the Red Empire. He has highly limited memory of the time. His parents moved to the Meridian Valley when he was young. Finley deeply distrusts the Red Empire and worries about it encroaching on the Valley.

Garnet Haze was a beauracrat in Finley's hometown, whom his parents fault for bringing down standards of living and labor rights. Haze was later discovered by the Mailers in present day to be complicit in Corvus Wright's [Human Trafficking Conspiracy](Corvin%20Hale.md#Human%20trafficking%20conspiracy).
