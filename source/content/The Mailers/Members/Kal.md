
## Vitals
**Pronouns**: He/Him
**Form:** Adapted
**Heritage**: [Corundrum](Red%20Empire)
**Background:** Labor - Worked for the Empire
## Appearance
Kal is a large man with pangoling scales on his body.

Kal wears baggy clothes and lumbers about, but he's quicker than you'd expect, at least when he needs to be. 

## Anchors
* "The people here want to help everyone, not just the elite"
* He won't admit it, but he's scared to wander again. He sees his friends as serving as a moral compass.

## Belief
"Without this community, I'll wander aimlessly or hurt people."
## Abilities
Kaliban is an [[Aegis]], a resolute protector.
### Playbook abilities
* **Intercepting Fist** - When you protect teammates or NPCs, take +1d to your resistance roll. When you collect info about possible threats to your team or NPCs in the current situation, you get +1 impact. 
* **Alloyed Mettle** -   When another character honestly confesses a fear or vulnerable emotion to you, you get +1 impact to reassure or comfort them. When you or another strider confess such feelings to each other, either of you can take 1 stress to clear 1 stress from the other.

## XP Triggers

* Did you address a challenge **with endurance** or **intercept danger before it could strike**?
* Heritage, background,, anchor, beliefs, and scars (like everyone)
